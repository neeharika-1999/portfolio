Key finding

MAML's gradient-based inner loop gives it a decisive edge in the hardest setting. In 5-way 1-shot tasks — where the model sees just one example per class — MAML's 84.3% accuracy outpaces Prototypical Networks by over 12 percentage points, demonstrating the power of optimising for fast adaptation rather than metric proximity.
